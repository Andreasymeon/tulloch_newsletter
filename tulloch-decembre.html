THE LAUNCH

I am delighted to introduce our first newsletter, celebrating the launch of our new watch, the T-01, First Edition.

- SHANE TULLOCH
FIRST EDITION, 50 PIECES

The T-01 First Edition is a special limited release of 50 individually-numbered pieces in 18k red and white gold.
TWO-TONE DIAL

Each piece has an alluring two-tone powder-frosted dial with embossed numerals carved from a single piece of 18k gold.
BUILT FROM THE GROUND UP

With a design inspired by precision chronometers from the roots of watchmaking history, and a movement built from the ground up at our workshop in Switzerland...
3 YEARS TO CREATE

The T-01 is the product of 3 years of research and development by a passionate team of experts in design, engineering and watchmaking.
